TikTok Money Maker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

TikTok Money Maker is a content automation tool used to draft, manage, and publish short-form videos. We collect only the data necessary to operate the service and complete TikTok integrations requested by the user.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process:

  • Account information such as email address and login identifiers
  • TikTok app credentials and authorization tokens required for integration
  • Content metadata such as video titles, descriptions, hashtags, and publication status
  • Operational logs needed to diagnose failures, monitor submissions, and improve reliability

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Authenticate users and connect authorized TikTok developer integrations
  • Prepare, upload, and manage short-form content on supported platforms
  • Track publication status and basic performance metrics
  • Maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot technical issues

3. TikTok Data

If you authorize TikTok features, we may access and store data provided by TikTok APIs only to support the requested publishing workflow. We do not sell TikTok user data and we do not use it for unrelated advertising purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers strictly as needed to host, secure, and operate the service. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect the integrity and security of the service.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate the service, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Users may request deletion of their account-connected data, subject to operational and legal constraints.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

TikTok Money Maker is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be reflected by the revised date at the top of this page.
