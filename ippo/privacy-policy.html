Ippo – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Our Philosophy

Ippo is built Privacy-First. Your health and location data never leaves your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

This policy explains how the Ippo mobile application ("the App") handles information when you use it on iOS or Android.

1. Data We Collect (Local Only)

All data generated by the App is stored exclusively on your device using a local database (SQLite). This includes:

  • Step count and distance per session
  • GPS coordinates of your walking route
  • Session timestamps and duration
  • Mood ratings and personal notes you choose to add
  • App settings and preferences

We have no access to this data. It is never transmitted to any server, and we do not use it for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

2. Location Data

The App requires access to your device's precise location (including background location) to:

  • Map your walking route in real time
  • Calculate distance traveled accurately
  • Continue tracking when the screen is off or when you switch apps

This location data is processed and stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Health Data

With your explicit permission, Ippo may interact with Apple HealthKit (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android) to:

  • Read step count data to improve session accuracy
  • Write walking session summaries (steps, distance, duration) to your health records

This integration is entirely local. Health data is processed on your device and governed by Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies. We never access your health data directly.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party — for any reason. Since we do not collect data on our servers, there is simply no data to share.

The App does not contain any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or third-party data collection libraries.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists on your device as long as the App is installed. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual sessions from the History screen within the App
  • Uninstalling the App (this permanently removes the local database)

6. Children's Privacy

Ippo is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no data is collected by us, this is not a concern in practice, but we note it for legal clarity.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be communicated through an update to the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
